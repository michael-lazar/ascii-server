Mozz - Personal ASCII

mozz/

My own humble collection of self-made ASCII art. Also mirrored on the nex:// protocol.

Textmode - Underground BBS ANSI

textmode/

A (partial) mirror of the 16colo.rs ANSI & ASCII archive. Contains pictures organized into "packs" and released by art groups over the span of decades. The archive is centered around the MS-DOS ANSI art but also contains Amiga ASCII and other legacy character sets which are collectively referred to as "textmode".

Fudan - Chinese BBS ANSI

fudan/

A translated mirror of the Sun and Moon Guanghua BBS at Fudan University in Shanghai, China. This BBS was a main hub of ANSI art for mainland China in the 2000's. There thousands of pictures cataloged by category and author, and other documentation including history, full-blown classes on ANSI art, and technical references.

AA - Japanese Shift_JIS

https://aa.mozz.us

A mirror of the website utf8art.com, which catalogs Japanese ASCII art (commonly referred to as just "aa") originating from the popular 2ch.net textboard.

The ASCII Art Emporium - Usenet ASCII

https://ascii.mozz.us:7070

A large collection of traditional 7-bit ASCII art, scraped from dozens of websites and converted into plain text files. Also includes an archive of alt.ascii-art and other Usenet newsgroups. This collection can also be downloaded as a single, compressed file from archive.org.
